Lao Papaya Salad (Tam Mak Hoong)

Tam Mak Hoong is a traditional Lao papaya salad known for its bold, spicy, salty, and sour flavors. It is a staple dish in Laos and is often eaten with sticky rice.

Ingredients

  • Green papaya, shredded
  • Garlic
  • Thai chili peppers
  • Fermented fish sauce (padek)
  • Fish sauce
  • Lime juice
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Optional: eggplant, crab, or shrimp

Instructions

  1. Pound garlic and chili peppers together in a mortar.
  2. Add tomatoes and lightly bruise.
  3. Add shredded papaya and gently pound while mixing.
  4. Add padek, fish sauce, and lime juice to taste.
  5. Mix well and adjust seasoning.
  6. Serve fresh with sticky rice.
